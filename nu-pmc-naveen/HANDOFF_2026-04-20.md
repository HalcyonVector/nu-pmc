## Known operational notes

### Rate limiting
- Login limiter: 10/15min per IP. During heavy automated testing, restart node to clear in-memory state.

### Sandbox quirks
- MariaDB dies between long idle gaps. If tests fail with "mysql socket not found," rebuild stack.
- Node process stays up longer but also can be killed between calls.

- Bugs #18-19 are low-severity super-user issues (Principal/DP reaching PMC-stage endpoint — they have authority, just confusing UX)
